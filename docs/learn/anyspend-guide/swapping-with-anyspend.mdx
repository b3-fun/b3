## How to Swap with AnySpend

Follow these steps to swap tokens using AnySpend:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Log into your AnySpend account on https://anyspend.com/ using your desired login option.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at5.17.10PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 17 10PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 17 10PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"65%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the token and chain to swap from">
    Choose the token and blockchain you want to swap out of.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at5.18.30PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 18 30PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 18 30PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"58%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select the token and chain to swap to">
    Choose the token and blockchain you want to receive.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at5.19.31PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 19 31PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 19 31PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"58%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter swap amount">
    Type in the amount of tokens you’d like to swap. When ready, click **Select a Payment Method**.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at5.21.26PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 21 26PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 21 26PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"59%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose payment method">
    Pick the payment method that works best for you.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at5.22.13PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 22 13PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 22 13PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"58%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Initiate the swap">
    Click **Swap** to proceed.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at5.23.21PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 23 21PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 23 21PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"58%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm payment method">
    - Pay directly from your connected wallet, or
    - Switch to **scan the QR code** / **manually send crypto**.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at5.24.39PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 24 39PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 24 39PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"59%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm in your wallet">
    Approve the transaction in your connected wallet. Once confirmed, your swap will process.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at5.25.36PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 25 36PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 10 01at5 25 36PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"60%" }}
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>
