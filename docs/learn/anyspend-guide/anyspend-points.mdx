---
title: "AnySpend Points System"
description: "Every swap you make with AnySpend earns you points. For every $0.01 swapped, you earn 1 point."
---

Points help you climb the leaderboard and unlock opportunities to earn \$ANY.

You can boost the amount of points you earn by engaging with supported features. Multipliers are applied when you:

- **Deposit** to HypeDuel
- **Bond** Arena Coins
- **Stake** supported tokens

![Screenshot2025 10 01at2 10 01PM Pn](/docs/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at2.10.01PM.png)

The more you engage with AnySpend, the higher you’ll rise on the leaderboard, and the more **\$ANY** you’ll earn when rewards are distributed.

### How to Check Your Points

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Points tab">
    From the navigation bar at the top of the AnySpend dashboard, click **Points**.

    ![Screenshot2025 10 01at4 17 03PM Pn](/docs/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at4.17.03PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Locate the active season">
    On the Points page, you’ll see your active season, which shows both your total points and your current leaderboard rank.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at4.18.19PM.png"

      title="Screenshot2025 10 01at4 18 19PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"82%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="View the leaderboard">
    From this page, you can also access the **Points Leaderboard** to see how you compare to other users.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at4.19.53PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 10 01at4 19 53PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 10 01at4 19 53PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"68%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="View your points history">
    Click **View points history** to see a breakdown of how your points were earned.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-10-01at4.20.50PM.png"

    In the points history, you’ll find details of each swap and activity that earned you points, including the date, token, and any multipliers applied.

    <img

  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Your total accumulated points are also displayed at the top right of the page, next to your profile icon for quick reference.
