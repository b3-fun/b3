### How to Submit an Appeal

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Support Chat">
    On the [docs.b3.fun](http://docs.b3.fun) website, click the chat bubble in the bottom-right corner to start a conversation with Support.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide Your Details">
    In your message, include your wallet address and a short note explaining that your account is restricted.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for Review">
    Once submitted, our Support team will review your account and follow up with you as soon as possible.
  </Step>
</Steps>
