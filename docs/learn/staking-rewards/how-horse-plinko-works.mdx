### How to Play Horse Plinko

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Horse Plinko">
    Go to the Staking Dashboard and choose **Horse Plinko** from the available games.

    ![Screenshot2025 09 25at4 06 51PM Pn](/docs/images/Screenshot2025-09-25at4.06.51PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Release Your Horse">
    Click **Release Horse** to start your play.

    ![Screenshot2025 09 25at4 04 13PM Pn](/docs/images/Screenshot2025-09-25at4.04.13PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign the Transaction">
    Confirm the action by signing the transaction in your connected wallet.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Receive Your Reward">
    Once the transaction is confirmed and the animation completes, your reward will be distributed automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
