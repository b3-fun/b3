---
title: "How to Stake B3"
description: "Step-by-step guide to staking your B3 tokens for passive rewards and access to Stake to Win games"
---

Staking your B3 tokens is quick and easy, and unlocks both passive rewards and access to our Stake to Win prize system. In this guide, we'll walk you through the simple steps to start staking directly on the platform.

## Option 1: Stake with B3 You Already Own

<iframe
  className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl"
  src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/Zu5TnpUBL6k"
  title="How to stake B3 tokens you already own"
  frameBorder="0"
  allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture"
  allowFullScreen
></iframe>

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to staking platform">
  Go to [stake.b3.fun](https://stake.b3.fun/) and sign into your account using your chosen login method.
</Step>

<Step title="Start staking process">
  Click **Stake more B3+**.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687f97cb6a4f6401251730bb/file-7lDhTXr2GU.png" alt="Stake more B3+ button on the staking dashboard" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Enter stake amount">
  Enter the amount you wish to stake and then click **Stake and Earn**.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687f97ea8518d2516d1a45e1/file-rzsRDBNoay.png" alt="Amount input field with Stake and Earn button" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm transaction">
  Confirm the transaction in your wallet to complete the process of staking your B3.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687f9a50185c133b2c0a48aa/file-G6t76GCh1z.png" alt="Wallet confirmation dialog for staking transaction" />
  </Frame>
  
  <Check>
    Your tokens are now staked and earning rewards!
  </Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Option 2: Swap & Stake from Other Tokens

<iframe
  className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl"
  src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/4tiZimJIv_M"
  title="How to swap and stake from other tokens"
  frameBorder="0"
  allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture"
  allowFullScreen
></iframe>

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to staking platform">
  Go to [stake.b3.fun](https://stake.b3.fun/) and sign into your account using your chosen login method.
</Step>

<Step title="Start staking process">
  Click **Stake more B3+**.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687f97cb6a4f6401251730bb/file-7lDhTXr2GU.png" alt="Stake more B3+ button highlighted" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Choose swap option">
  Then click **Swap & stake** from any crypto.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687f9fccb28ecf16639c863b/file-QKSATdeFEt.png" alt="Swap & stake option for converting other tokens" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Set amount">
  Enter the amount you wish to stake, and then click **Continue**.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687fa28f185c133b2c0a4901/file-w4b8RL8Tg4.png" alt="Amount input with Continue button" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Select source token">
  You'll then be able to select the token and chain you want to swap from.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687fa22b965d8266feba1fa0/file-S02D7Kb8YN.png" alt="Token and chain selection interface" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Review and checkout">
  Once you've made your selection, click **Checkout**.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687fa334b28ecf16639c8651/file-MS2DQQAAiS.png" alt="Checkout button for swap and stake transaction" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Complete payment">
  Next, click **Pay from Connected Wallet** (or you can choose to pay from a different wallet), and then confirm the transaction in your wallet to complete your swap+stake.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687fa3656a4f640125173121/file-cyWnP0BTYw.png" alt="Payment method selection and final confirmation" />
  </Frame>
  
  <Check>
    Your tokens will be swapped to B3 and automatically staked, earning rewards immediately!
  </Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The minimum staking amount is 50 B3. Once staked, your tokens will immediately start earning passive rewards and unlock access to Stake to Win games.
</Note>
