---
title: "Depositing Tokens on B3"
description: "Learn how to bridge and deposit tokens from other networks to B3 using the built-in bridge interface"
---

To interact with some games and features in the B3 ecosystem, you'll need tokens in your wallet. This guide walks you through how to deposit tokens using the B3 bridge.

## How to Deposit Tokens

<iframe
  className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl"
  src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/-99zipMlOlQ"
  title="How to deposit tokens to B3"
  frameBorder="0"
  allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture"
  allowFullScreen
></iframe>

<Steps>
<Step title="Log in to basement.fun">
  Log in to your account at basement.fun.
</Step>

<Step title="Select network and token">
  Select the network and token you want to deposit to B3.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687dfe81bb2dcf4c62e168fe/file-cwmhuEFxD0.png" alt="Network and token selection interface" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Enter amount and bridge">
  Enter the token amount you want to deposit and click **Bridge**.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687dff266828a66bbecf0bff/file-35Fdwt0jZC.png" alt="Token amount input with Bridge button" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm transaction">
  Confirm the transaction in your connected wallet to complete your deposit.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687e04626828a66bbecf0c0b/file-Cz781bdyhB.png" alt="Wallet confirmation dialog for deposit transaction" />
  </Frame>
  
  <Check>
    Once the transaction is complete, your tokens will appear in your wallet.
  </Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Deposits typically complete within a few minutes, but processing times may vary depending on network congestion.
</Note>
