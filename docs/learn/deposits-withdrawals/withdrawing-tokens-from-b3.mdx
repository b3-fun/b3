---
title: "Withdrawing Tokens from B3"
description: "Learn how to securely withdraw your tokens from the B3 network back to Ethereum or other supported chains"
---

Need to move your tokens out of the B3 network? Whether you're bridging back to Ethereum or another supported chain, withdrawing from B3 is simple and only takes a few steps. This guide walks you through how to securely withdraw your assets.

## How to Withdraw Tokens

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to the bridge">
  Log in to your account at [basement.fun/bridge](https://basement.fun/bridge).
</Step>

<Step title="Select withdrawal tab">
  Locate and click the tab labeled **Withdrawal**.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687e4730357cee4756b4cf06/file-VJ2PNGPlJ5.png" alt="Withdrawal tab in the bridge interface" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Choose destination network">
  Scroll down and select the network you would like to withdraw your tokens to.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687e4768a66e62177ed6d8f1/file-HybSHUyoMY.png" alt="Network selection dropdown for withdrawal" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Select token">
  Select the token you want to withdraw.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687e482abb2dcf4c62e16a10/file-ulOP7Uh4gi.png" alt="Token selection interface" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Enter amount and bridge">
  Enter your withdrawal amount, and then click **Bridge**.
  
  <Frame>
    <img src="https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/687dff266828a66bbecf0bff/file-35Fdwt0jZC.png" alt="Amount input field with Bridge button" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm transaction">
  Confirm the transaction in your wallet to complete your withdrawal.
  
  <Check>
    Once confirmed, your tokens will be bridged to the selected network and should arrive shortly.
  </Check>
</Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Withdrawal processing times typically range from a few minutes to an hour, depending on network congestion and the destination chain.
</Info>
