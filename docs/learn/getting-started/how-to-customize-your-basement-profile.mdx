Your Basement profile is your digital identity on the Basement platform. You can customize your avatar, choose a unique name, and display a badge that represents your style. Whether you're setting things up for the first time or updating your look later, personalizing your profile helps you stand out and feel more connected.

## Customizing Your Profile for the First Time

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the account menu">
    Click your avatar in the top right corner to open the account menu.

    <Frame>
      ![User avatar in top right corner of interface](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/6878eb85522f451361e7f954/file-tG5tN1GNTT.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Register your name">
    Enter your desired name, and then click **Register**.

    <Frame>
      ![Name registration dialog](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/6878ec1a13d950405eff6e87/file-WPlAB5IC8k.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize your avatar">
    Next, you'll be guided through customizing your avatar. This is your chance to make it truly yours by choosing a gender, adjusting physical features, and picking the outfit that best fits your style.

    <Frame>
      ![Avatar customization interface showing various options](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/6878ed8552724d3c54de9062/file-Q6M0LQjVdu.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save your avatar">
    Once you're happy with your avatar, click **Next** to save your virtual self.

    <Frame>
      ![Final avatar preview with Next button](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/6878edcb1fd2e3184b5c2fe5/file-jqWk6jdnO8.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Additional Customization

After your initial setup you can return to your profile anytime to make changes or try something new.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access your profile">
    From the Basement homepage click the profile icon on either the left or right side of the page to open your profile and begin editing.

    <Frame>
      ![Profile icons on Basement homepage](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/6878ef3752724d3c54de906e/file-kvphfoR7j2.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open customization options">
    Next, click **Customize Profile**.

    <Frame>
      ![Customize Profile button in profile menu](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/6878efb113d950405eff6e9d/file-4y3VMWALKJ.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Available Customization Options

**Username**
Change your public display name at any time

**Avatar**
Edit your avatar's appearance to refresh your look

**Express Yourself**
Add a custom hand-drawn signature and choose a profile theme that matches your vibe
