---

You can sign up using an EVM wallet, email, phone number (SMS), or a social account like Google, Apple, Discord, Telegram, or Farcaster.

## Step-by-Step: Creating Basement Account

<iframe className="w-full aspect-video rounded-xl" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/kGIAFx63Oyg" title="How to Create a B3 Account" frameBorder="0" allow="accelerometer; autoplay; clipboard-write; encrypted-media; gyroscope; picture-in-picture" allowFullScreen />

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to basement.fun">
    Go to [basement.fun](http://basement.fun) and click **Join the fun**.

    <Frame>
      ![Basement homepage with Join the fun button highlighted](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/6875028d03e72d42ff87c5e8/file-u8mlvi8Bn3.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose your login method">
    Choose your preferred login method. You can click **More options** to see additional login methods.

    - **EVM Wallet** (e.g. MetaMask or Rainbow)
    - **Email or Phone Number (SMS)**
    - **Social Account** (Google, Apple, Discord, Telegram, or Farcaster)

    <Frame>
      ![Login options modal showing different authentication methods](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/6875033f03e72d42ff87c5ee/file-YAotVXCkm3.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your login method">
    Follow the prompts to confirm your login method. This might include connecting your wallet, verifying your email, or approving access through a social account.

    <Note>
      Example: If you're using an EVM wallet like Metamask, you'll see a screen like this when confirming your connection:
    </Note>
    <Frame>
      ![MetaMask connection confirmation dialog](https://s3.amazonaws.com/helpscout.net/docs/assets/685306b72e0550277aa9250e/images/6875053a0cf7d86089ec497e/file-uvVfmUrTYQ.png)
    </Frame>
    <Check>
      Once confirmed, your B3 account will be created and ready to use across all B3 platforms.
    </Check>
  </Step>
</Steps>
