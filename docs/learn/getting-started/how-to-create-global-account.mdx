### Creating Your B3 Global Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Explorer">
    Go to https://explorer.b3.fun/.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Sidebar">
    On the left side of the page, hover to expand the sidebar. Select any option (such as **Transactions** or **Browse Apps**) to reveal the sign-in option.

    ![Screenshot2025 09 24at6 23 08PM Pn](/docs/images/Screenshot2025-09-24at6.23.08PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect Your Account">
    In the top-right corner of the screen, click **Connect**.

    ![Screenshot2025 09 24at6 26 24PM Pn](/docs/images/Screenshot2025-09-24at6.26.24PM.png)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a Login Method">
    - **Wallet (MetaMask, WalletConnect, etc.)** - Approve the connection request in your wallet.
    - **Social / Email / Phone** - Follow the verification process appropriate for your login method (e.g., email confirmation, SMS code, or social login authorization).

  </Step>
</Steps>
