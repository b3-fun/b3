---
title: "How to Manage Linked Accounts in Your B3 Global Account"
description: "You can link multiple login methods (wallets, email, phone, or social accounts) to your B3 Global Account for added flexibility, and disconnect them at any time."
---

### Linking an Additional Account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign In">
    Log in with the account you want to link new accounts to.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Click your profile icon in the top-right corner, then select **Settings**.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-09-24at6.44.13PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 09 24at6 44 13PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 09 24at6 44 13PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"54%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Link a New Account">
    Click **Link New Account**.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-09-24at6.47.56PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 09 24at6 47 56PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 09 24at6 47 56PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"66%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Account Type">
    Choose whether to link a wallet, email, phone, or social account.

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-09-24at4.58.36PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 09 24at4 58 36PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 09 24at4 58 36PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"38%" }}
    />
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Connection">
    Follow the steps to approve the connection (wallet approval, email verification, SMS code, or social sign-in).

    <img
      src="/docs/images/Screenshot2025-09-24at4.59.29PM.png"
      alt="Screenshot2025 09 24at4 59 29PM Pn"
      title="Screenshot2025 09 24at4 59 29PM Pn"
      className="mx-auto"
      style={{ width:"44%" }}
    />
  </Step>
</Steps>

Your additional account will now be linked.
