## Brand Guidelines

<Note>
**Make it nostalgia.**

Invoke a sense of childlike-play.

**Make it fun.**

Keep things light, friendly, and engaging.
</Note>

The BSMNT brand should evoke:
- **Nostalgia** - Connect with fond gaming memories
- **Playfulness** - Childlike wonder and excitement
- **Fun** - Light-hearted and enjoyable experiences
- **Accessibility** - Friendly and welcoming to all users

## Usage Guidelines

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Logo Usage">
- Maintain proper spacing around logos (minimum clear space equal to the height of the "B" in BSMNT)
- Don't stretch, skew, or alter the proportions of logos
- Use appropriate contrast ratios for accessibility
- Don't place logos over busy backgrounds that compromise readability
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Color Usage">
- Primary purple should be the dominant brand color
- Use the full color palette to create vibrant, engaging designs
- Ensure sufficient contrast for text readability
- Feel free to experiment with additional fun, gaming-inspired colors
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Typography">
- San Francisco is preferred for body text and UI elements
- Experiment with display fonts that evoke nostalgia and playfulness
- Maintain readability across all device sizes
- Consider gaming-inspired fonts for headers and special elements
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
