## How It Works

1. **Browse Sites** - Explore crypto content sites with traffic stats
2. **Pick Your Slot** - Choose banner size and time slot
3. **Upload Your Ad** - Provide image URL and landing page
4. **Pay with USDC** - One signature, done - no gas fees
5. **Go Live** - Your ad appears immediately at scheduled time
